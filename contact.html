Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple landing page might take 1-2 weeks, while a full website could take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline estimate after discussing your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

+

Yes! I offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website updated, secure, and performing optimally. This includes content updates, security monitoring, and technical support.

What's included in your web development service?

+

My services include responsive design, cross-browser compatibility, SEO optimization, performance optimization, accessibility compliance, and thorough testing. I also provide training on how to manage your content.

Do you work with existing websites?

+

Absolutely! I can help improve existing websites through redesigns, performance optimization, adding new features, or migrating to modern technologies. I'll assess your current site and recommend the best approach.
